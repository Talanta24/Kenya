Final Assessment of IMF & World Bank's Role in Kenya

A balanced evaluation of their impact on the economy.

Weighing the Pros and Cons

The IMF and World Bank have played a significant role in shaping Kenya’s economic landscape. While their interventions have led to growth and development, they have also introduced challenges. This evaluation provides a comprehensive look at their impact.

✅ The Benefits

Infrastructure development, financial stability, and economic support during crises.

⚠️ The Challenges

Debt burdens, economic inequality, and restrictive policies limiting national sovereignty.

Final Thoughts

Kenya’s relationship with the IMF and World Bank is complex. While their involvement has driven progress, the country must also be mindful of long-term financial independence. Moving forward, strategic policies and sustainable economic plans are essential.
